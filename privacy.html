Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At Rao's Chat X, we are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. We store your profile information and messages securely in Firebase, and your email is used solely for authentication purposes.

We want to emphasize that we do not share your personal data with any third parties. Your information is used exclusively to provide you with the best chatting experience possible.

Data We Collect

To provide our services, we collect and store the following information:

  • Profile Information: Your name, username, gender, age, and profile avatar
  • Authentication: Your email address for account verification and login
  • Messages: Your chat conversations to enable seamless communication
  • Media: Profile pictures stored in Firebase Storage
  • Activity Data: Online status and last seen information

Data Security

We utilize industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure email/password authentication
  • Firestore Database: Encrypted storage for Users, Chats, and Reports
  • Firebase Storage: Secure media storage for avatars

Data Control & Deletion

You have full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact the developer directly.

Contact for Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please reach out to the developer through the app's contact options. We will process your request promptly and confirm once all your data has been removed from our systems.
